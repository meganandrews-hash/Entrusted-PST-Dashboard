Tech Guide: How to Justify Your Equipment

  • Grain Depression (Dehumidifier Efficiency): The Dehu Outlet GPP must be lower than the Chamber GPP. This proves the machine is actively pulling water out of the air. (e.g., Chamber is 88 GPP, Dehu Out is 38 GPP = 50 GPP Grain Depression. This proves the dehu is working!)
  • Air Mover Sizing: Calculate 1 air mover per inset/offset greater than 18 inches, plus the standard square footage calculations for flooring and walls.
  • Dehumidifier Sizing (AHAM): Log the exact Cubic Footage (L x W x H) and the Class of Water to prove the AHAM pint capacity installed matches the IICRC S500 requirements.
